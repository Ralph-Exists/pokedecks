Computer Search

________________________________________


This card is played by discarding 2 cards from your hand to search your deck for 1 card in your deck.
By playing this card, you are able to put nothing to chance and gain an advantage against your opponent.



Having originated in the first ever set of the Pokemon TCG, Base Set it was a requirment to have 4 in your deck or end up lagging behind.
It was so broken that even Wizards Of The Coast (who published and maintained the game during its early days) could not stop it's reign of terror.

Fun Fact: Because the Computer Search Ace Spec has the same text, it is considered a reprint of the Base Set card, so the Base Set card can legaly be used as long as it is used as an Ace Spec in your deck!
